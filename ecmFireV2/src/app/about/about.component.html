ABOUT US:

From the beginning:

Our entry into the equestrian community in 2009 began as a result of a young girl's love of ponies. It has since grown into a passion for the development and management of show horses with an emphasis on hunter, jumper and equitation disciplines.

EILEEN McDONNELL has been "groom" at her daughter Claire's side, all while serving as CEO at a major U.S. life insurer. Since "giving up the reins" in corporate America, Eileen has been focused on further strengthening their equestrian roots.

What Differentiates ECM:

Care and connectedness is a part of the goods and services that our horses experience as a part of daily life. Don't be surprised when you come to visit with us that our horses enthusiastically offer welcoming greetings.

At ECM, you'll find that all horses have been treated with love and compassion, which ultimately impacts their performance.

That loving care extends to every aspect of compassionate and consistence in managing, training and producing our award-winning horses.
